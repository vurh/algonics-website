Project Highlights

Proven delivery across critical sectors

From nuclear facility power systems to solar-IoT research — delivering complex, custom-engineered solutions.

BHAVINI — Dept. of Atomic Energy

Space Vector Modulated Variable Frequency Drive

Two-level SVM-based VFD for asynchronous induction machines at the PFBR facility, Kalpakkam. Delivered with on-site training and simulation. Algonics is an approved vendor for power electronics, UPS, drives and HMI for the PFBR project.

IIT Mandi — SERB IMPRINT-2

WatEr-ERIS: Solar IoT Irrigation System

Joint research MoU for solar-powered, cloud-based automated irrigation. Designed IoT modules, solar panels, sensors and water pump integration with real-time data acquisition across Himachal Pradesh and Tamil Nadu.

Bidirectional AC-DC Power Converters

IGBT-based 11kW regenerative bidirectional converters (48A, 220V DC) with <3% ripple and ±2% stability. Also delivered phase-controlled variable frequency dual AC sources (47.5–52.5 Hz).

Our Approach

Where power hardware meets intelligent software

Deep power electronics expertise combined with modern software engineering — enabling truly integrated, IoT-connected energy systems.

01

Digital Power Electronics

DSP-controlled IGBT/MOSFET switching topologies. Proven firmware IP: PID, FFT, FIR, IIR, space vector modulation. Systems from 100W to 100kW+.

02

IoT, HMI & Sensors

Custom SOC-based IoT with RTOS, browser-based HMI, and sensor networks. Isolated voltage/current feedback to cloud-connected SCADA dashboards.

03

Cloud & Applications

Cross-platform web & mobile apps, Python/Django backends, MongoDB, Redis, big data pipelines. Billing, payment and fleet management for EVSE.

04

Custom Magnetics & Hardware

In-house magnetic design with simulation tools. Complete power stack assembly — switching devices, snubbers, DC link capacitors, gate drivers, thermal management.
